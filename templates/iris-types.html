Types of Iris Flowers

  • Iris Setosa: Typically small, with short petals and sepals.
  • Iris Versicolor: Medium-sized with variable petal and sepal sizes.
  • Iris Virginica (or Iris Virginica): Larger flowers with longer petals and sepals.
